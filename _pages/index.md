I am a graduate student in Computing Science at the University of Alberta, working with [Dr. Martha White](https://webdocs.cs.ualberta.ca/~whitem/) on reinforcement learning research as part of the [RLAI Lab](https://rlai.ualberta.ca/) and [Amii](https://www.amii.ca/). I am currently working with the FAI Combinator Team at Google DeepMind Montreal.

My research interests focus on deep reinforcement learning, LLM post-training, and continual learning. I also have prior experience working on computer vision and NLP systems, with applications in generative AI, diffusion models, LLMs, RAG pipelines, and multi-modal agent systems.
